Senior Players Profile

Player Info:

SRU: This stands for Scottish Rugby Union, which is the unique identification number assigned to each player registered with the SRU. It helps in tracking the player's history and credentials within the rugby union.

Personal Details:

Known Health Issues: This field is crucial for recording any medical conditions or allergies the player has. It ensures that our medical team is aware of any potential health risks and can provide appropriate care.

Address Details:

Address Line 1 and Line 2: These fields capture the player's residential address. "Address Line 1" is for the primary part of the address (e.g., house number and street name), while "Address Line 2" is for any additional address information (e.g., apartment number or suite).

Emergency Contact Details:

Emergency Contact Name and Number: These fields are used to record the contact details of someone who can be reached in case of an emergency involving the player. This person should be able to provide critical information and support during urgent situations.

Doctor Information:

Doctor Name and Contact Number: This information is vital in case medical records or further health details are needed quickly. Having the contact details of the player's doctor can expedite the process of obtaining necessary medical history or advice.

Game Profile

Game Info:

Game Name: This field captures the name or title of the game, providing a unique identifier for each match.

Home Team: The name of the team playing at the home venue.

Opposition Team: The name of the opposing team.

Start Date and End Date: These fields denote the start and end dates of the game, providing a timeframe for the match.

Location: The venue or location where the game is taking place.

Kickoff Time: The scheduled kickoff time for the match.

Result: The outcome of the game (e.g., win, loss, draw).

Score: The final score of the game.

Additional Details: This section allows for any extra information relevant to the game, such as scores, comments, etc.

Note:
For each game, there can only be two halves.

Game Details:

Home Score: The score of the home team at the end of the game.

Home Comment: Any comments or notes regarding the home team's performance.

Opposition Score: The score of the opposition team at the end of the game.

Opposition Comment: Any comments or notes regarding the opposition team's performance.

Session Info:

Session Name: This field captures the name or title of the training session, providing a unique identifier.

Squad Participating: The squad or team members participating in the training session.

Additional Details: This section allows for any extra information relevant to the training session, such as specific skills or any injuries or accidents.

Session Activities:

Skills: Skills practiced during the training session.

Activities: Activities practiced during the training session.

Present Players:

Accidents: Any accidents or incidents that occurred during the training session.

Injuries: Any injuries sustained by players during the training session.
